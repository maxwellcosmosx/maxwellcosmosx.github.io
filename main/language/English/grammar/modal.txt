== shall
=========
Shall I vp. ? 	我能..吗？		#询问对方的许可#
Shall we vp. ? 	我们一起..吧? 	#提出建议#
shall vp.		必须..			#用在正式规定之中#

=========
== will
=========
will vp. 单纯的将来，心里没有计划
The meeting will start at 9 AM.

will vp. 当即状态下，表示"去做.."
A: "The phone is ringing!"
B: "I will answer it."

will never 绝不会
I will never lie to you. 

I/we will vp. 我们/我承诺..
We will finish the project on time. 

will vp. 准会..	对未来事件走向的推测
I think she will like the gift.

will you vp. 你愿意..吗?
Will you marry me? 

** won't vp. 就是不 	#超级地道/超级重要#
No matter what I do, she just won't pick up my calls.

Wouldn't you vp. ? 	你不..吗？

would vp.		过去常常..	My grandfather would always tell me stories.
My grandfather would always tell me stories.

would like to vp.

would rather vp.

must vp.		肯定是..			#肯定推测#

must vp.		必须..			#硬性规定#

You must vp.	强烈推荐你..		#强烈推荐#
You must visit that museum—it’s amazing!
