=========
== shall
=========
shall vp.		必须..			#用在正式规定之中 比must更正式#
The tenant shall pay the rent on the first day of each month. 

Shall I/we vp. ? 	我们一起..吧? 	#提议#
Shall we begin the meeting?

shall 必将
We shall never surrender. 
I shall return.

=========
== should
=========
should 应该(建议)
People should be kind to each other.

should 按理应该(推测)
It's 9:00. She should be at work by now.

should've

shouldn't have

=========
== will
=========
will vp. 单纯的将来，心里没有计划
The meeting will start at 9 AM.

will vp. 当即状态下，表示"去做.."
A: "The phone is ringing!"
B: "I will answer it."

will never 绝不会
I will never lie to you. 

I/we will vp. 我们/我承诺..
We will finish the project on time. 

will vp. 准会..	对未来事件走向的推测
I think she will like the gift.

will you vp. 你愿意..吗?
Will you marry me? 

** won't vp. 就是不 	#超级地道/超级重要#
No matter what I do, she just won't pick up my calls.

=========
== would
=========
would you + vp. ?
Would you pass me the salt, please?

Would you mind vp_ger. ?	你介意..吗？
Would you mind closing the door?

Would you like sth. ?		你想要..吗？
Would you like some coffee?

What would you do if...
What would you do if you were me?

Wouldn't you vp. ? 	你不..吗？
Wouldn't you prefer tea instead?

I would say ...
I would say it’s a good idea.

would vp.		过去常常..
When I was a kid, my grandfather would tell me stories before bed.

would like to vp.
I would like to order now. 

would prefer to

would love to

would rather vp.
She would rather stay home. 

would vp. 有可能
This approach would likely yield better results.

wouldn't 绝不会
I wouldn't say he’s the best candidate. 

if 		, would ..
If I had more time, I would learn Japanese.

if had 	, would have ..
If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.

would've

wouldn't have


=========
== can
=========
can 能（能力）
I can speak three languages.
can - be able to
could - was able to
[X] - will be able to
[X] - have been able to
[X] - had been able to

can 能（有可能）
It can get very cold here in winter.

Can I/We... 请求他人的许可
Can I ask you a question?

can 表示许可
Sure, you can borrow my car.

can't vp. 		绝对不可能..		#否定推测#
That can't be true!
That can’t be John at the door. He’s in London this week.
He can't have missed the train—it's still on the platform.

can + smell/see/hear (代替进行时，使描述更生动)
I can hear someone knocking at the door.


I can't believe... 不敢相信..
I can’t believe I passed the exam!


=========
== could
=========
Could I ask a quick question? 礼貌请求

If you’re tired, you could take a nap. 给出委婉的建议 完全没有说教感

It could rain later, so take an umbrella.（推测，不太确定）

could've

couldn't have

How could you? 失望与责备

I couldn’t agree more. 我同意得不能再同意

I couldn’t care less. 我一点都不在乎

=========
== may
=========
may .. 可能 #书面语，口语中might#
This may be the best solution we have.

May I ... 我能..吗?
May I borrow your pen, please? 

You may.. 你可以..了
You may begin the exam now. 

May you both live happily ever after! （祝你们永远幸福！）【祝词】

May the Force be with you. （愿原力与你同在。）【台词】

May God bless you. （愿上帝保佑你。）【祷词】

=========
== might
=========
might .. 可能 #最常用最地道#
Take an umbrella. It might rain later. 

We might .. 我们不妨
We might try calling their customer service. 

You might wanna .. 你或许需要.. 非常委婉得体的建议
You might want to back up your files before the update. 

might as well 干脆就..吧 #来都来了的劝诱语感#
Since you're here, you might as well stay for dinner. 


=========
== must
=========
mustn't vp.		禁止..
You mustn't eat here. 

must vp.		肯定是..			#肯定推测#
She must be tired. 
The floor is wet—it must have rained last night. 

must vp.		必须..			#硬性规定#
You must fasten your seatbelt while driving.

You must vp.	强烈推荐你..		#强烈推荐#
You must visit that museum—it’s amazing! 

I must vp. 		我必须得..		#个人决心#
I must finish this report tonight.
